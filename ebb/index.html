TEXT LOG: THE OFFER

[Author: Ebb ████████████]

June 18 1995

I have just returned from QuantumCon, where I encountered an unusual number of individuals willing to discuss Quantum Physics without ridicule. Among them was a man calling himself Vale.

He was… interesting. His face appeared damaged, unstable, almost as if it were a holographic image rather than a physical form. I chose not to question it. His understanding of the Quantum Theorem was overwhelming.

Vale claimed to have worked alongside Einstein himself on an experimental project a machine designed to stabilize a wormhole. One large enough to allow instantaneous travel to virtually any location.

We spoke for hours. Before we parted, he offered me a position at the Georgia Research Institution. It seems he was once a highly regarded figure there.

Relocating to Georgia will be difficult. It is far from my current residence. Still, I could not reject an opportunity of this magnitude. I have already begun packing and secured a rental near the Institute for the duration of my recruit period.

Vale suggested that, should I prove myself capable, I may earn the title of Doctor even look after subordinates who would assist my research and attend my lectures.

I believe I have been exceptionally fortunate. For that I am grateful.
